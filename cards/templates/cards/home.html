How It Works?

Flashcards are a powerful tool for learning and memorisation. The process is simple:

You start with flashcards in Box 1. When you know the answer to a flashcard, it moves up to the next box (e.g., Box 2). The goal is to move flashcards through the boxes until they reach Box 5.

If you answer a flashcard incorrectly at any point, the card moves back to Box 1, giving you another chance to study it. This method helps reinforce information you are struggling with, while moving cards you're confident about up to the highest level.
